BIM Publishing Service


Problem

We found out from our clients there was a need to share BIM models (in 3D) also to stakeholders who don't have access to any modelling software nor technical skills to use any 3D software. Tridify already used internally technology and automation workflows that would enable this. Task was to design an online service that would enable our clients to share BIM models and viewed easily by everyone. Also purchasing the service online was needed.


Solution

A web service where clients could upload and publish their models to a viewable link. A user friendly web viewer was needed to view BIM models by a broad target audience. We prototyped many ideas and conducted usertests using online platforms like usertesting.com and uerlytics. In addition to UX design of the service I helped out marketing department in taking into use new tools like hubspot, google analytics/tag manager. Also designing and implementing our wordpress marketing site.

  • Client: Tridify
  • Year: 2018-2020
  • Role: UX design, Usability, Marketing Automation

VR interior designer






Problem

Tridify wanted a VR integration for their interior design application. The interior application was designed for tablets and desktop and it includes a lot of features. The task was to find main use cases for VR, design and implement them together with a VR developer.


Solution

Started the project by user story mapping and creating a list of features that would be needed to design an interior in VR. We then benchmarked and analyzed interesting VR games and other applications that could be found for HTC Vive. The design process included creating fast prototypes of each interaction and testing them internally in the company and with people visiting the office. The chosen interaction methods was finetuned and implemented into the final product.

  • Client: Tridify
  • Year: 2018
  • Role: Consept design, Benchmarking, Interaction design, Usability

Interior design app





Problem

Tridify had created an Interior design application for tablets, desktop & mobile. The application had been designed/developed feature by feature for years and usability was no more optimal. The task was to find main usability issues and re-design the application.

  • Year: 2018
  • Role: Design, User interviews, Usability

Posti lähettäminen


Problem

Posti is the leading postal and logistics service company in Finland. Postis ambition was and still is to move traditional touchpoints into digital world. Our Ux researchers had already discovered needs and a high level consept how to further digitalize Postis traditional parcel sending. My task was to take the consept into next level and design the user interface of the service.


Solution

At Posti there is many stakeholders needed to take into account at design phase. Best way to present the flow and user interface was by prototyping.
