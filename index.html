BIM Publishing Service



Background

Clients expressed a need to share BIM models (in 3D) with stakeholders who don’t have access to modeling software or the technical skills to use 3D tools. The task was to design a service where clients could easily share BIM models with anyone, anywhere. The viewing experience needed to be intuitive for non-technical people while also offering advanced features for professional users. In addition, better collaboration tools were needed to enable smooth communication between stakeholders in construction projects directly within the shared 3D models.


Solution

A web service was developed to allow clients to upload their BIM models and publish them as shareable, viewable links with communication tools. The viewer needed to be customizable to suit different use cases and stakeholder needs. A user-friendly 3D web viewer was essential for making BIM models accessible to a broad non-technical audience. We prototyped multiple concepts and conducted user testing via online platforms.

www.tridify.com

  • Client: Tridify
  • Year: 2018-2020
  • Role: UX design, User testing, Marketing

VR interior designer






Background

Tridify wanted a VR version of their interior design application. The interior application had been designed for tablets and desktop and it includes a lot of features. The task was to find main use cases for VR, design and implement them together with a VR developer.


Solution

The project began with user story mapping and compiling a list of features required to design an interior in VR. We then benchmarked and analyzed notable VR games and other VR applications. The design process involved ideating, rapidly prototyping each required interaction method, and testing them both internally within the company and externally with office visitors. The selected interaction methods were then refined and implemented into the final product.

  • Client: Tridify
  • Year: 2018
  • Role: Consept design, Benchmarking, Interaction design, Usability

Posti lähettäminen


Background

Posti is the leading postal and logistics service company in Finland. Postis ambition was and still is to move traditional touchpoints into digital world. Our UX researchers had already identified user needs and developed a high-level concept for further digitalizing Posti’s traditional parcel-sending process. My role was to elevate this concept and design the service’s user interface.


Solution

At Posti, multiple stakeholders had to be considered during the design phase. The most effective way to present the flow and interface was through prototyping, so my task was to design and build an HTML prototype that allowed users to interact with the UI as if it were a live service. While developing the MVP, we also designed additional features for future iterations. The prototype was refined iteratively, and Posti’s brand guidelines were followed throughout.

  • Year: 2014
  • Role: UI design, prototyping
